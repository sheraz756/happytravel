How it works

1 You Contact Me Whether you’ve found a destination or need inspiration, as your Personal Travel Agent I will take the time to find out what makes your perfect trip. Keeping your interests and price range in mind.

2 I Get To Work… Using my expert knowledge, years of travel experience and personal contacts across hundreds of travel companies, I’ll do all the hard work for you.

3 I’ll Look After Everything… Once you’re happy with the holiday, I then personalise it! I can organise everything from car parking, transfers and even activities to make your holiday as stress free and enjoyable as possible.

4 You Sit Back & Relax… I run my travel business in partnership with your Co-op and I book package holidays which are financially protected through ABTA & ATOL, so you can relax knowing you’re in safe hands.
